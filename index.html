Our Mission

At VAST, our mission is to transform the way people perceive and utilize coffee waste by providing an innovative solution that makes repurposing spent coffee grounds easy. We empower individuals to dry and reuse their coffee grounds for a variety of sustainable applications, reducing waste and promoting eco-friendly practices in everyday life.

Our Product

Our product is a compact, 3D-printed solution designed to effortlessly dry spent coffee grounds, preventing mold and unpleasant odors. Its airflow system ensures even drying, allowing users to repurpose coffee waste for gardening, cleaning, and skincare.
